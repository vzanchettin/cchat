#### RTC registers:
	*   00    current second in BCD
	*	02    current minute in BCD
	*	04    current hour in BCD
	*	06    day of week  in BCD
	*	07    day of month in BCD
	*	08    month in BCD
	*	09    year  in BCD (00-99)
